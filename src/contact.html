Studio Strip is a new multi-use space located in the hub of Amsterdam Noord, close to Central Station, public transport and popular cafe’s such as Garage Noord and Skatecafe. Studio Strip offers a photo studio to suit your photography needs with the conveniences of parking spots in front of the door. The creative space is available for a wide array of collaborations and offers a unique industrial workspace character with indoor lighting and access to studio equipment to suit your work. The studio is ideal for all sorts of shoots or other activities such as an art space, exhibition space, conference room and live stream events.
